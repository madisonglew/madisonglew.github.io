Product Management


“The real role of the product manager in the organization is to work with a team to create the right product that balances meeting business needs with solving user problems,” – Melissa Perri, Escaping the Build Trap.

During my time at Autodata Solutions (a division of J.D. Power & Associates), I have worked on our automotive insurance, automotive media and most recently, automotive incentives products. While these products revolve around different market segments, they all have the common theme of helping the automotive industry market vehicles to end-consumers in some fashion. Our automotive insurance product, StudyPRICE, focuses on which vehicle safety features are most likely to decrease your overall driving risks, and intern, your automotive insurance cost. For vehicle incentives, our product brings to light all the available cash back options and financing rates allowing the end-consumer to get into their car for the best deal. And lastly, our media products help to visually showcase a dealers vehicles and help them decide what vehicle is ultimately best for them and their family.


Automotive Incentives – LenderDesk, Chrome Incentives Service & Incentives Data Extracts
Check out our product page >>

As the product manager on our automotive incentives team, we are working to develop a solution to support the automotive digital retail segment from start to finish. We are focused on simplifying the automotive merchandising process through creating the best deal scenarios (highest cash back for cash purchases and lowest monthly payments for lease and loan transactions) for end-consumers. At the end of the day, consumers want a simple way to get into a new vehicle while maximizing their cash back or minimizing their monthly payments. With the shift in consumer behaviour to subscription-based payment services and purchases, digitizing the automotive payments to process for the end-consumer is of utmost importance. Through the simplification of automotive incentives and increasing the ease of use through pre-determining the best stackable combination of incentives programs, LenderDESK and CIS are increasing transparency for end-consumers by telling them what payments options are available for them and their lifestyle.


Automotive Media – VINpx, Chrome Image Gallery & Vehicle Animations
Check out our product page >>

Our media products focus on showcasing vehicles and vehicle features to the end consumer through the use of imagery, videos & animations. When consumers are shopping for a new vehicle, media is one of the first things they see at the beginning of the sales funnel. Ultimately, our media helps users understand the key features of a vehicle and helps them to visualize themselves actually driving the vehicle.

As the PM for our media products, I focus on ease integration and delivery of our media products to our customers and ensuring our media is syndicated out to all the correct resources. Daily, we syndicate more than 250,000 media assets to website providers and social media platforms.


Automotive Risk Analysis – StudyPRICE
Check out our product page >>

StudyPRICE our risk analysis product targeting insurance companies to help them better understand features available on a vehicle, if the feature is installed or an optional upgrade and how the feature influences the overall safety of the vehicle. Insurance companies use this information to access the overall risk of their current portfolio, in addition to using the data to assess liability and influence quotes of new customers and their vehicles.

Our most recent feature developments have been around flagging vehicle features as ADAS (explain the acronym) features and mapping vehicle features to correct image angles.
